Aircraft through different Generations

A view through the different eras of aviation

Piston engine fighters

When Piston powered fighters appeared in WW1 , these platforms were considered a new technology that is yet to be fully meeting its potential , which then became a major military asset in WW2 with the introduction of long rang bombers, and they certainly spearheaded the evolution of Aircraft as whole.

First Generation

The First of their kind, these platforms were a first major breakthrough on aviation after they started being used in combat operations, with the use of the jet powered engine which was hundreds of kilometers faster than the fastest propellers at that time capable of outpacing and out-manaveruing anything in the air, certainly scared anyone who saw them scream by in the air.

Second Generation

The start of new era in combat ,where jet powered fighter are now a much needed asset in any combined arms operation ,with it came the fast development of aircraft ,and their weapons, The era of Supersonic flight has just began.

Third Generation

Almost as fast as 2nd generation platforms came ,3rd generation ones were already in the works ,and with it came much needed improvements in reliability and weapons systems and much faster speeds, with some 3rd generation platforms still being in use to this day.

Fourth Generation

The golden generation, which has brought may iconic aircraft that still represent the bulk of most modern fleets and with constant upgrades and improvements it doesn't look like its going to change anytime soon.

Fifth Generation

The future is here, the age of the fifth generation aircraft is fast approaching but its not certain you can spot it in time, with the new but curial element of stealth that ensures you can't see it until its too late.
